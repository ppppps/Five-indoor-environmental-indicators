# Five-indoor-environmental-indicators
This Dataset rely on five indicators to judge whether there are people in the room. 
The five indoor environmental indicators are outdoor temperature, indoor temperature, oxygen concentration, nitrogen concentration and carbon dioxide concentration. The final data 1 represents someone, 0 represents nobody.
There are three txt files, one for train and two for test. 
datatraining.txt has 8143 pieces of data.
datatest.txt has 1665 pieces of data.
datatest2.txt has 9752 pieces of data.
